Game Characters

Miss Scarlett - Known for her beauty and cunning.

Professor Plum - An intellectual with a questionable past.

Colonel Mustard - A military man with a secret.

Mrs. White - The housekeeper with hidden motives.

Miss Peacock - A socialite with a sharp tongue.

Mr. Green - A businessman with a shady past.

Game Rooms

Ballroom - A grand room where many mysteries unfold.

Kitchen - Holds many secrets, especially around dinner time.

Library - A quiet place filled with books and shadows.

Study - Where plans are made and secrets are kept.

Conservatory - A glass house filled with plants and light.

Hall - The entrance to the manor with many stories.

Weapons

Candlestick - A classic weapon of choice.

Revolver - A deadly firearm used in many mysteries.

Dagger - A sharp blade with a history.

Lead Pipe - Heavy and effective for a sneak attack.

Rope - A versatile tool for nefarious deeds.

Wrench - Useful for both repairs and mischief.

Game Rules

The objective of Clue is to solve the murder mystery by deducing who did it, with what weapon, and in which room.

  • Each player takes turns rolling the dice.
  • Players move around the board to different rooms.
  • Make suggestions about the murder to gather clues.
  • Keep track of clues to deduce the answer.
